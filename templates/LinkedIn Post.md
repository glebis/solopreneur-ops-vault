# LinkedIn Post Template

## Hook (first 2 lines — visible before "see more")

[Use a pattern from [[frameworks/LinkedIn Hook Patterns]]]

**Options:**
- "I [did X] for [duration]. Here's what [happened]."
- "Stop [common advice]. Here's what matters instead."
- "How to [outcome] in [time] ([constraint])."

## Body (150-300 words)

- One core insight, not three
- Specific numbers and examples
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences)
- No links in body (put in first comment)

## Closing

- Ask a question OR share a takeaway
- No "what do you think?" — too generic
- Better: "What's your version of this?" or "The part most people skip is..."

## First Comment

- Link to resource (if any)
- Relevant hashtags (3-5 max)
- Tag 1-2 people mentioned (if appropriate)

## Checklist

- [ ] Hook passes the "would I click see more?" test
- [ ] No external links in body
- [ ] 80% educational, 20% promotional at most
- [ ] Specific numbers included
- [ ] Under 300 words
