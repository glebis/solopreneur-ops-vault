# 5-Minute Wins

> The smallest action that moves the needle. Every morning, guaranteed.

## Principle

From [[frameworks/Behavioral Activation]]: the hardest part is starting. A 5-minute win removes the activation energy barrier completely.

## The Catalog

### Visibility (1-3 min each)
- Comment on 1 relevant LinkedIn post with insight
- Share a skill demo screenshot with one-line caption
- Reply to a DM with a helpful link
- Repost someone's content with a short take

### Engagement (2-5 min each)
- Send a follow-up to a warm lead
- Thank someone who shared your content
- Introduce two people who should know each other
- Ask a genuine question in a relevant community

### Content (5-10 min each)
- Record a 2-minute Loom of one skill in action
- Write 3 LinkedIn post drafts (headlines only)
- Turn an agent research output into a quick post
- Screenshot a cool result and add context

### Sales (3-5 min each)
- Review one agent-researched lead profile
- Send one "saw this, thought of you" message
- Update one case study with recent data
- Draft one sentence of a testimonial request

## Rules

1. Dashboard always has at least one 5-min item
2. Do the 5-min item BEFORE opening Telegram
3. After completing, mark done — streak visible
4. If you do only the 5-min item today, that's enough
