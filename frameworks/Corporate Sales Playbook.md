# Corporate Sales Playbook

> How to sell AI training to companies without a sales team.

## Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Duration | For |
|------|-------|----------|-----|
| Half-day workshop | €2,500 | 4 hours | Awareness, intro |
| Team program | €10,000-15,000 | 2-4 weeks | Skill building |
| Enterprise per-seat | €1,200/person | 6 weeks (lab format) | Large teams |
| White-label delivery | €1,000-1,500/day | Custom | Via consultancies |

## Revenue Impact

From [[research/Corporate AI Training]]: one deal/quarter at €10K = **€40K/yr** extra.
Combined model reaches **€72-92K/yr** — above the €84K comfort target.

## 5 Paths to Corporate Deals

1. **Alumni referral** — graduate recommends you to their company (highest conversion)
2. **LinkedIn inbound** — L&D leader sees your content, reaches out
3. **Conference speaking** — 56% capture rate, present → follow up
4. **Consultancy partnerships** — they sell, you deliver
5. **Direct outreach** — targeted DACH companies (lowest conversion, highest volume)

## Budget Cycles

| Quarter | Best For |
|---------|----------|
| Q1 (Jan-Mar) | New year budgets, fresh allocations |
| Q2 (Apr-Jun) | Mid-year training programs |
| Q3 (Jul-Sep) | H2 budgets, autumn programs |
| Q4 (Oct-Dec) | "Use it or lose it" budget, year-end training |

**EU AI Act enforcement Aug 2, 2026** creates urgency for Q3-Q4 2026.

## The Compliance Angle

From [[research/EU AI Act Compliance]]: Art. 4 mandates AI literacy training. €7.5M penalty for non-compliance. Frame your workshop as **compliance + capability** — not just nice-to-have.
