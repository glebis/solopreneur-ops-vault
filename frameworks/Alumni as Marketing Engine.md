# Alumni as Marketing Engine

> 350+ alumni are your best sales team — if you activate them.

## The Facilitator School Model

From the case study: two solo facilitators built €250K+ in cohort revenue. Their #1 discovery channel: **alumni sharing certificates and results on LinkedIn**.

Key numbers:
- Some certificate posts got 100+ engagements
- 56% capture rate at conferences where alumni spoke
- Most enrollments came in final 1-2 days (urgency from alumni testimonials)

## How to Activate Alumni

### 1. Make Sharing Easy (5 min setup)
- Create a shareable certificate/badge for lab completion
- Pre-write a LinkedIn post template alumni can customize
- Include a branded hashtag (#ClaudeCodeLab)

### 2. Showcase Results (ongoing)
- Feature alumni projects in your Thursday "proof posts"
- Quote alumni in your sales conversations (with permission)
- Create a "wall of builds" on the Astro site

### 3. Create Referral Incentives
- Alumni discount for referring new participants
- Free month of community for successful referral
- Early access to new skills/content for active promoters

### 4. Community → Cohort Pipeline
- Alumni community (157 members) is the warm pool
- Community members see new cohort announcements first
- "Bring a colleague" pricing for team enrollment
