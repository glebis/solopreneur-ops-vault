# Content Flywheel

> Content generates audience. Audience generates content ideas. The loop sustains itself.

## From Linear to Circular

[[frameworks/Content Leverage]] captures the 1→7 ratio. This framework captures the **loop**:

```
Create (lab session)
    ↓
Publish (YouTube + LinkedIn + blog)
    ↓
Audience grows (followers, subscribers)
    ↓
Audience asks questions / shares problems
    ↓
Questions become new content ideas
    ↓
Create (addressing real questions)
    ↓
Publish...
```

## Content as Infrastructure

Content isn't marketing — it's infrastructure. Each piece serves multiple functions simultaneously:

| Content Piece | Marketing | Sales | SEO | Community | Corporate |
|--------------|-----------|-------|-----|-----------|-----------|
| YouTube demo | Visibility | Social proof | Indexed | Discussion topic | Proposal attachment |
| LinkedIn post | Reach | Lead magnet | n/a | Shareable | Credibility |
| Blog/vault page | SEO | Reference | Indexed | Resource | White paper |
| Case study | Trust | Conversion | Indexed | Inspiration | Required by procurement |

## Acceleration via Agents + Voice

From [[research/Voice-First Workflows]]: dictation is 3.75x faster than typing.
From [[MOC/Agent Ops]]: agents handle decomposition (1→7).

Combined: **voice dictate a 10-min debrief → agent produces 7 content pieces → publish across channels → audience responds → dictate response → repeat.**

Total active time: ~30 min/week for 7+ pieces.
