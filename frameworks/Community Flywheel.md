# Community Flywheel

> Every member interaction is simultaneously delivery, content, social proof, and referral.

## The Mechanism

```
Member joins
    ↓
Asks a question → You answer (DELIVERY)
    ↓
Answer becomes a post → (CONTENT)
    ↓
Post shows expertise → (SOCIAL PROOF)
    ↓
Member shares badge on LinkedIn → (REFERRAL)
    ↓
New person sees → joins community
    ↓
Asks a question...
```

Unlike the [[frameworks/Infinite Sales Cycle]] (the macro loop across all channels), the flywheel is the **micro engine inside the community**. Each interaction powers all four functions at once.

## Metrics That Prove the Flywheel Works

| Metric | Signal | Target |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Questions/week | Engagement health | 10+ |
| Answers by members (not you) | Self-sustaining | >50% |
| LinkedIn badge shares/month | Referral engine | 5+ |
| New members from referral | Flywheel spinning | 30%+ of new |
| Retention at 90 days | Delivery working | >75% |

## Flywheel Accelerators

From [[research/Community Pricing]]:
- **Weekly live session** — single highest retention signal
- **Annual pricing** — 92% retention vs 68% monthly
- **5-Day Challenges** — conversion event that seeds the flywheel with new energy
- **Certificates** — badge sharing on LinkedIn closes the referral loop

## Break-Even

8 members at €49/mo = €392/mo (covers Circle platform cost + time).
At 50 members: €2,450/mo recurring.
