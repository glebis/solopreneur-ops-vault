# Business Metrics

> The numbers that tell you if your business is working. Update monthly.

## Unit Economics

| Metric | Formula | Current | Target |
|--------|---------|---------|--------|
| **CAC** | Marketing spend / New customers | ~€0 (organic) | <€100 |
| **LTV** | Avg revenue × Avg retention | ~€950 (1 cohort) | €2,500 (cohort + community) |
| **LTV:CAC** | LTV / CAC (target >3:1) | ∞ (organic) | >10:1 |
| **Payback Period** | CAC / Monthly revenue per customer | Instant | <1 month |

## Revenue

| Metric | Formula | Current | Target |
|--------|---------|---------|--------|
| **MRR** (community) | Members × Price | 0 × €49 = €0 | 50 × €49 = €2,450 |
| **Cohort Revenue** | Students × Price / Quarter | ~8 × €500 = €4,000/q | 8 × €950 = €7,600/q |
| **ARR** | (MRR × 12) + (Cohort × 4) | ~€16,000 | €29,400 + €30,400 = €59,800 |
| **Revenue/hour** | Monthly revenue / Hours worked | ? | >€50/hr |

## Runway

| Metric | Formula | Current |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **Monthly burn** | Rent + tools + insurance + tax reserve | ~€4,000 |
| **Break-even** | Burn / Revenue per unit | 5 individual students or 2 teams |

## How to Update

1. Fill "Current" values on the 1st of each month
2. The [[agents/Dashboard Curator]] creates a monthly metrics task
3. Track trends over time — sparklines on [[MOC/Sales Pipeline|Sales Pipeline]]
4. Review in [[frameworks/Infinite Sales Cycle|Infinite Sales Cycle]] context

## Key Thresholds

- **LTV:CAC > 3:1** → growth is sustainable
- **Churn < 5%** → community is healthy
- **Revenue/hour > €50** → pricing is right
- **Leverage ratio > 1:5** → content engine is working
- **Break-even ≤ 5 students** → business model is viable
