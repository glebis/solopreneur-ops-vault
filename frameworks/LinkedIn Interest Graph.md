# LinkedIn Interest Graph (2026)

> LinkedIn now shows your posts to people who care about your topic — not just people who know you.

## The Shift

Before March 2026: LinkedIn distributed posts to your **connections** (Social Graph).
After March 2026: LinkedIn distributes posts based on **topic relevance** (Interest Graph).

This is a massive opportunity for solopreneurs: you don't need 10,000 connections to reach the right people. You need **consistent expertise signals** on a specific topic.

## How It Works

```
Your post → Quality check → 8-15% of connections see it
                          → Algorithm measures: dwell time, saves, comments
                          → If signals strong → expanded to non-connections
                                                interested in your topic
```

## What This Means for You

1. **Profile = Semantic Entity**: LinkedIn cross-references your content with your profile skills, job title, and posting history to determine your "expertise areas"
2. **Consistency beats virality**: 3 posts/week on AI agents > 1 viral post + silence
3. **Saves > likes**: A save signals "I want to come back to this" — the strongest educational content signal
4. **Comments must be substantive**: Multi-sentence comments that add insight count 10x more than "great post!"

## Action Steps

- Align profile skills with content topics: AI Agents, Claude Code, Automation, Workshop Facilitation
- Post consistently on 2-3 topics max (don't scatter)
- Optimize for dwell time: longer posts, carousels, videos with captions
- Never include links in the post body (penalty) — add links in first comment
