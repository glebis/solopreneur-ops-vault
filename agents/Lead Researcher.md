# Lead Researcher

> When someone connects, know who they are before you reply.

## What It Does

Researches a new LinkedIn connection's profile, company, recent posts, and potential fit for lab/community. Produces a 1-paragraph briefing.

## Output

Briefing added to the relevant task or saved as `tasks/lead-brief-[name].md`.

## Information Gathered

- Role, company, company size
- Recent posts (topics, engagement level)
- Mutual connections or shared interests
- Likely budget range for team/individual enrollment
- Suggested opening message angle

## Autonomy Level

**L2 (Copilot)** — agent researches, human decides whether and how to reach out.
