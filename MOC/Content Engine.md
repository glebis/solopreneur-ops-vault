## Content Types

### High-Leverage (create weekly)
- Skill demo videos (5-10 min) → YouTube + LinkedIn
- Deep research outputs → Blog/Vault posts
- Lab session highlights → Social clips

### Medium-Leverage (create monthly)  
- Case studies from lab participants
- Framework explanations with examples
- Tool comparison guides

### Agent-Generated (continuous)
- Market scans and trend reports
- Curated opportunity lists
- Translated/adapted content for markets

## Distribution Map

```
Studio recording
    ├── YouTube video
    ├── LinkedIn post (key insight)
    ├── Blog post (detailed)
    ├── Newsletter excerpt
    └── Telegram channel post
```
