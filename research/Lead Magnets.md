# Lead Magnets for Solo AI Educators (2026)

> From generic PDFs to AI-native experiences that ARE the product demo. Choose one this week, launch it next week.

## 1. Lead Magnet Landscape 2026

The static PDF era is over. In 2026, 91% of B2B buyers prefer interactive content over static formats. The shift is dramatic:

### Conversion Rates by Type

| Type | Avg Conversion | Best For | Effort |
|------|---------------|----------|--------|
| **AI-adaptive quiz/assessment** | 38-47% | Qualification + lead capture | Medium |
| **Interactive calculator/tool** | 30-40% | Immediate perceived value | High |
| **Webinar (live)** | 20-40% (to qualified lead) | Trust building + demo | Medium |
| **Short video (personalized)** | 15-25% | Engagement + personality | Medium |
| **Checklist / cheat sheet** | 10-20% | Quick wins, high volume | Low |
| **Guidebook / e-book** | 5-15% | SEO + authority | High |
| **Generic PDF** | 3-5% | Don't bother | Low |

**Key insight:** Education landing pages convert at a median 8.4% — one of the highest across all industries. With an AI-native lead magnet, you can push this to 25-40%.

### What's Saturated vs. What Still Converts

**Saturated (avoid):** Generic "Ultimate Guide to AI" PDFs, basic email courses, downloadable slide decks.

**Still converts:** Interactive assessments that give personalized results, free tools that solve a real problem, templates people can use immediately, live demos where you build something in real-time.

## 2. AI-Native Lead Magnets

The killer advantage for an AI educator: your lead magnet can USE AI, making it both valuable AND a product demo.

### The "AI Agent Readiness Assessment"

**Concept:** 10-question quiz → personalized report on which tasks in the respondent's workflow are best suited for AI agents. Includes a score, priority ranking, and suggested first agent to build.

**Why it works:**
- Conversion: 38-47% (quiz category)
- Captures: role, company size, pain points, current tools (natural lead scoring data)
- Output IS the value: a personalized roadmap, not generic advice
- Demonstrates exactly what the lab teaches

**Implementation:** ScoreApp or Typeform with conditional logic → generates report → email with full results + CTA to lab/community.

### The "Paste Your Workflow, Get an Agent Design"

**Concept:** Text input field where someone describes a repetitive workflow. An agent analyzes it and returns a structured agent design: what it would do, which tools it needs, estimated time savings.

**Why it works:**
- Feels like magic (personalized, instant, useful)
- Captures the lead's actual problem (gold for sales conversations)
- The output naturally leads to "want to build this? Join the lab."

**Implementation:** Astro page on your site with a form → Claude API call → structured response. Cost: ~€0.05 per analysis.

### The "Context Interview" as Lead Magnet

You already have [[templates/Context Interview]]. Reposition it:

**Current:** Template in the vault (passive, requires download).
**Upgraded:** Interactive web form on solo.salient.community/start → generates personalized context.md → emailed to them → demonstrates how agents use personal context.

**Conversion path:** Take interview → receive context.md → "Now imagine every agent reads this. Want to set that up? Here's the community."

This is unique to you and deserves its own strategy:

## 5. Lead Magnet → Conversion Funnel

### The 5-Email Nurture Sequence

After someone downloads a lead magnet, send 5 emails over 14 days:

| Email | Day | Subject Pattern | Goal |
|-------|-----|----------------|------|
| 1 | 0 | "Here's your [resource]" | Deliver + first impression |
| 2 | 2 | "The one thing most people miss about [topic]" | Teach + build trust |
| 3 | 5 | "How [alumni name] built [specific thing] in 6 weeks" | Social proof |
| 4 | 9 | "A question for you" | Engagement + segment |
| 5 | 14 | "If you're serious about [outcome]" | Soft CTA to community/lab |

**Benchmarks to aim for:**
- Open rate: 40-50% (education niche is high)
- Click rate: 5-8%
- Unsubscribe: <2% per email
- Conversion to community/lab: 2-8% of sequence completers

### The "Give First" Philosophy

How much to give away before asking:

**Rule of thumb:** Give away the WHAT and WHY freely. Charge for the HOW and WITH-WHOM.

- **Free:** "AI agents can automate your LinkedIn engagement" (what)
- **Free:** "Here's why most people fail at agent automation" (why)
- **Paid:** "Build your LinkedIn agent step by step with a cohort" (how + community)

63% of leads convert within 3 months when they receive regular nurturing. Don't rush the ask.

## 6. Lead Scoring for Solopreneurs

You don't need a CRM. You need frontmatter.

### Vault-Based Lead Scoring

Create a `templates/Lead.md` with Dataview-queryable fields:

```yaml
---
type: lead
name: 
company: 
role: 
source: # linkedin | conference | website | referral
score: 0
signals:
  downloaded_skill: false
  completed_interview: false
  attended_challenge: false
  visited_pricing: false
  replied_to_email: false
status: cold # cold | warm | hot | customer
last_contact: 
---
```

### Scoring Rules

| Signal | Points | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| Downloaded free skill | +5 | Interest in tools |
| Completed Context Interview | +15 | High engagement, shared personal data |
| Attended 5-Day Challenge | +20 | Invested time and €29 |
| Visited pricing page | +10 | Commercial intent |
| Replied to nurture email | +10 | Engagement |
| Referred by alumni | +15 | Social proof + warm intro |
| Role: L&D/innovation leader | +10 | Corporate buyer signal |
| Company size >50 | +10 | Corporate budget likely |

**Thresholds:** Cold (0-15) → Warm (20-40) → Hot (45+) → Reach out personally.

```dataview

FROM "leads"
WHERE status != "customer"
SORT score DESC
LIMIT 10
```

## 8. The "Skill as Lead Magnet" Model

### Which Skills to Open-Source

**Criteria for free skills:**
- Immediately useful in under 5 minutes
- Demonstrates a concept taught in the lab
- Works standalone (no dependencies on premium skills)
- Has a natural "want more?" moment

**Suggested free skills:**
1. A simple vault initializer
2. A content repurposer (1 input → outline)
3. A basic LinkedIn post drafter
4. A meeting summarizer
5. A quick research skill

**Keep premium:**
- Complex multi-agent workflows
- Domain-specific skills (genomics, finance, design)
- Skills that require extensive prompt engineering
- Skills that integrate multiple MCP servers

### "Built with Claude Code Lab" Footer

Add to every free skill's SKILL.md:

```
---
Built with [Claude Code Lab](https://solo.salient.community)
50+ skills. 350+ alumni. Hands-on AI agent training.
---
```

### Tracking Installs

- GitHub stars/forks as popularity signal
- Include optional anonymous telemetry: `curl -s https://solo.salient.community/api/ping?skill=NAME` on first run
- Track referral traffic from GitHub to site via UTM parameters
