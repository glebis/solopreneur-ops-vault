## 1. Freelancer Visa (Aufenthaltstitel)

### Freiberufler vs. Gewerbe: Which Are You?

As an **AI educator** running labs and courses, you likely qualify as **Freiberufler** under §18 EStG — specifically as a *Lehrer* (teacher) or *unterrichtende Tätigkeit* (educational activity). This is the best classification because:

- No Gewerbesteuer (saves 7–17% of profits)
- No Gewerbeanmeldung required
- No IHK membership fees
- Simpler bookkeeping (Einnahmen-Überschuss-Rechnung, not double-entry)

**Risk:** If the Finanzamt considers your activity commercial (e.g., selling digital products at scale rather than teaching), they may reclassify you as Gewerbe. Selling recorded courses or digital products leans Gewerbe; live instruction and consulting leans Freiberufler. A Steuerberater can advise on keeping the classification.

### Visa Requirements (§21 AufenthG)

For non-EU citizens applying in Berlin:

1. **Viable income proof** — client contracts, letters of intent, or evidence of existing revenue
2. **Ertragsvorschau** — projected monthly earnings/expenses spreadsheet (12 months)
3. **Health insurance** — private (PKV) typically required for initial visa; public (GKV) possible if previously employed in Germany
4. **Business plan** — activity description, target clients, revenue model
5. **Qualifications** — relevant education or portfolio demonstrating expertise
6. **Passport** with at least 6 months validity

**Processing:** 6–10 weeks at Ausländerbehörde Berlin. Fee: €75–€100. Initial permit: up to 3 years, renewable.

**Key tip:** The Ausländerbehörde wants to see that your business benefits the German economy. For education, emphasize: training German/EU workforce in AI skills, bringing international clients/revenue to Berlin, contributing to Berlin's tech ecosystem.

Sources: [Make it in Germany](https://www.make-it-in-germany.com/en/working-in-germany/setting-up-business/visa/freelance), [All About Berlin](https://allaboutberlin.com/guides/freelance-visa), [Remote Work Europe](https://remoteworkeurope.eu/insights/germany-freelance-visa-non-eu-2026/)

## 2. Tax Structure

### Income Tax (Einkommensteuer)

Germany uses a **progressive formula** (not fixed brackets). Key thresholds for 2026:

| Taxable Income | Effective Rate | Tax on This Amount |
|---------------|---------------|-------------------|
| €0 – €12,348 | 0% | €0 |
| €12,349 – €17,443 | 14–24% (progressive) | ~€500–€1,200 |
| €17,444 – €69,879 | 24–42% (progressive) | Gradual rise |
| €69,880 – €277,825 | 42% flat | Spitzensteuersatz |
| €277,826+ | 45% flat | Reichensteuer |

**Plus:** Solidaritätszuschlag (5.5% of income tax, but only if tax exceeds ~€18,130).

**Example:** At €60,000 taxable income, expect ~€15,000–€16,000 income tax (~25–27% effective rate).

**Filing deadline:** July 31, 2027 for 2026 income. With Steuerberater: end of February 2028.

### VAT (Umsatzsteuer)

**Kleinunternehmerregelung (§19 UStG):**
- Exempt if previous year revenue ≤ €25,000 AND current year ≤ €100,000
- No VAT on invoices, but also no Vorsteuerabzug (input VAT deduction)
- Threshold based on **actual net turnover** (not estimates, as of 2025 reform)

**If you exceed the threshold or opt out:**
- Standard rate: 19% (7% for certain educational services)
- **B2B within EU:** Reverse charge — you invoice without VAT, buyer handles it. Requires buyer's valid VAT ID on invoice.
- **B2C within EU:** VAT at customer's country rate. Use OSS (One-Stop-Shop) to file in one place.
- **B2C outside EU:** Generally VAT-free export of services
- **B2B outside EU:** VAT-free

**For live online education specifically:** Since 2025, virtual events/courses to B2C EU customers are taxed at the customer's location. Use OSS to avoid registering in every EU country.

**Practical recommendation:** If revenue is under €25,000, use Kleinunternehmerregelung for simplicity. Once you scale past it, register for VAT and use OSS for EU B2C sales.

Sources: [Taxfix](https://taxfix.de/ratgeber/selbststaendige/kleinunternehmergrenze/), [Norman Finance](https://norman.finance/blog/kleinunternehmer), [Commenda](https://www.commenda.io/blog/europe-vat-guide-for-digital-content-creators)

## 4. Legal Structure Options

### Comparison for Scaling

| Feature | Freiberufler | UG (haftungsbeschränkt) | GmbH |
|---------|-------------|------------------------|------|
| **Setup cost** | €0 (Finanzamt registration) | €1,300–€2,200 | €27,000–€29,500 |
| **Minimum capital** | None | €1 (practical: €500+) | €25,000 |
| **Setup time** | 1–2 weeks | 4–8 weeks | 6–10 weeks |
| **Liability** | Personal (unlimited) | Limited to company assets | Limited to company assets |
| **Gewerbesteuer** | No | Yes (above €24,500) | Yes (above €24,500) |
| **Bookkeeping** | EÜR (simple) | Double-entry (Bilanz) | Double-entry (Bilanz) |
| **Credibility** | High for consulting | Medium | High for B2B |
| **Scaling** | Hard (personal service) | Natural path to GmbH | Full corporate structure |
| **Best for** | Solo teaching/consulting under €100k | Testing product-market fit | Scaling past €100k with employees |

### Recommended Path

1. **Start as Freiberufler** — zero setup cost, simplest taxes, no Gewerbesteuer
2. **If selling digital products at scale** → consider UG to separate liability and preserve Freiberufler status for consulting
3. **If hiring or exceeding €100k+ in product revenue** → UG converts to GmbH when retained profits reach €25,000

**Hybrid approach:** Many Berlin solopreneurs maintain Freiberufler status for consulting/teaching AND run a UG for product sales. Two tax returns, but tax-optimal.

Sources: [Norman Finance — UG vs GmbH](https://norman.finance/blog/ug-vs-gmbh), [Norman Finance — Freiberufler oder Gewerbe](https://norman.finance/blog/freiberufler-oder-gewerbe), [betahaus](https://www.betahaus.com/magazine/how-to-start-a-business-in-germany-2026)

**Recommended combo for international education business:**

- **Wise Business** — multi-currency receiving (USD, GBP, EUR) for international clients
- **Finom** or **sevDesk** — invoicing with German compliance (Pflichtangaben)

### Invoicing Requirements (Germany)

German invoices must include:
- Full name and address (you and client)
- Tax number (Steuernummer) or USt-IdNr (VAT ID)
- Invoice number (sequential)
- Invoice date and delivery date
- Line item description, quantity, unit price
- Total net + VAT (or "Gemäß §19 UStG wird keine Umsatzsteuer berechnet" for Kleinunternehmer)

**Tools:** sevDesk (€8.50/mo), lexoffice (€5.90/mo), Finom (built-in), or Kontist (basic).

## 6. Health Insurance

### The Choice: Public (GKV) vs. Private (PKV)

**As a self-employed person, you can choose either — regardless of income.**

#### Public (Gesetzliche Krankenversicherung)

- **Rate:** 14.6% + ~2.9% Zusatzbeitrag + 3.6% Pflegeversicherung = ~21.1% of assessed income
- **Minimum contribution** (if low income): ~€230/mo
- **At €60k income:** ~€850–€950/mo (you pay employer + employee share)
- **Pros:** Family members covered free (Familienversicherung), rate stays same as you age, easier to return to employment
- **Cons:** Expensive at higher incomes, basic coverage, long waits for specialists

#### Private (Private Krankenversicherung)

- **Cost:** €400–€700/mo (age 30–40, basic-comprehensive)
- **Pros:** Faster specialist access, broader coverage, cheaper than GKV if young and healthy
- **Cons:** Premiums rise with age (significantly after 50), no family coverage (each person pays separately), very hard to switch back to GKV after 55
- **For visa:** Often required/preferred for initial Freiberufler visa

#### Recommendation

| Situation | Choose |
|-----------|--------|
| Under 35, healthy, no kids planned soon | PKV — cheaper now, better coverage |
| Planning family, want long-term stability | GKV — Familienversicherung saves thousands |
| Income fluctuates significantly | GKV — contributions adjust with income |
| Want to return to employment later | GKV — avoids the PKV trap |
| Need visa approval quickly | PKV — simpler for Ausländerbehörde |

Sources: [Feather — PKV 2026](https://feather-insurance.com/blog/private-health-insurance-threshold-yearly-changes), [Germanpedia](https://germanpedia.com/health-insurance-for-self-employed-and-freelancers-germany/), [Health Insurance in Germany](https://www.health-insurance-in-germany.com/health-insurance/self-employed/)
