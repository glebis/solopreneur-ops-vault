# Partnership Channels for AI Education Distribution in Europe

> Beyond organic LinkedIn — structured channels that bring students to you without cold outreach.

## 1. Accelerators & Incubators in Berlin/DACH

Berlin has 30+ active accelerators. The ones with the strongest AI training needs:

| Program | Focus | Partnership Angle | Timing |
|---------|-------|------------------|--------|
| **AI NATION Accelerator** | AI startups, 6-month program | Offer agent-building workshops during the program. They run mandatory in-person workshops Apr–Sep 2026. | Applications: Jan–Feb annually |
| **Berlin Startup School** | Early-stage, generalist | AI module as part of their curriculum. They accept external workshop facilitators. | Rolling |
| **Merantix AI Campus** | 80+ AI teams, 6,000m² campus | Host workshops in their event spaces. Natural audience of AI practitioners. Already hosts AI Tinkerers meetups. | Ongoing — apply for event hosting |
| **Science & Startups** | University spinoffs | Guest lectures + workshop series. Ranked Top 2 university startup hub in Germany (FT 2026). | Academic calendar |
| **GTEC (German Tech Entrepreneurship Center)** | Corporate-backed | AI training for corporate innovation teams. They partner with external trainers. | Q1/Q3 cohorts |

**How to approach:** Email the program manager directly with a 1-page proposal: "I'll run a free 90-minute Claude Code workshop for your current cohort. 350+ alumni, hands-on from minute one." The free workshop becomes the lead magnet for paid cohorts.

**Expected conversion:** 5-15% of workshop attendees → lab interest. At 20 attendees per workshop, that's 1-3 leads per event.

## 8. Revenue Sharing Models

| Model | Typical Split | Best For | Risk Level |
|-------|--------------|----------|-----------|
| **Affiliate/referral** | 10-20% of enrollment | Bootcamps, communities, alumni | Low — pay only on conversion |
| **Revenue share** | 60-80% to content creator, 20-40% to partner | Coworking spaces, event venues | Medium — partner has distribution |
| **White-label licensing** | €500-2,000/month flat fee | Consultancies, corporates | Low — predictable income |
| **Co-branded program** | 50/50 split | Universities, accelerators | Medium — shared reputation |
| **Speaker fee** | €500-3,000 per talk | Conferences, corporate events | Low — upfront payment |
| **Free workshop → pipeline** | 0% (investment) | All channels | High short-term, high long-term |

**Recommended starting model:** Free workshops at 2-3 venues (Merantix, Factory, one accelerator) → measure conversion → scale with revenue share at venues that convert.

White-label partnerships grew 32% across B2B sectors in 2025 (Gartner). The trend is accelerating — consultancies want to offer AI training without building content.
