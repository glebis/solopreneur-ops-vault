> "Free AI courses from Anthropic Academy are genuinely good — I'd recommend them to anyone who wants foundational knowledge. But 'comparable content' doesn't produce comparable outcomes, and that's worth naming directly.
>
> The structural reason: self-paced courses optimise for completion, not for behaviour change. You learn what the video says. You pass the quiz. You get the certificate. You feel informed. >
> For practitioners who are genuinely self-directed and just need the conceptual map: take the free Anthropic Academy courses. They're excellent for that. For practitioners who want to change how they work — to actually use these tools in their operations, not just know what they are — the cohort structure is what makes that change stick.
>
> The question to ask yourself: have you already completed a free AI course and then not changed how you work? That's your answer."
